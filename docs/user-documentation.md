# Leave Manager Plugin: End-User Guide

**Author:** Manus AI
**Version:** 2.0

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the Leave Manager plugin! This guide will walk you through how to use the plugin to manage your leave requests, view your leave balance, and stay up-to-date with your team's schedule.

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1. Logging In

To get started, you will need to log in to the leave management system. Your administrator will provide you with the URL for the login page.

1.  Navigate to the login page.
2.  Enter your username and password.
3.  Click **Login**.

If you have forgotten your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

### 2.2. Your Dashboard

Once you are logged in, you will be taken to your dashboard. The dashboard provides a quick overview of your leave information:

- **Your Leave Balance:** See how many days of leave you have remaining for each leave type.
- **Upcoming Leave:** View your upcoming approved leave.
- **Pending Requests:** See the status of your pending leave requests.
- **Team Calendar:** Get a glimpse of who in your team is on leave.

## 3. Managing Your Leave

### 3.1. Applying for Leave

To apply for leave, follow these steps:

1.  Navigate to the **Apply for Leave** page.
2.  Select the **Leave Type** you are requesting (e.g., Annual, Sick).
3.  Choose the **Start Date** and **End Date** for your leave.
4.  If you are taking a half-day, you can specify whether it is the morning or afternoon.
5.  Provide a **Reason** for your leave request.
6.  Click **Submit Request**.

Your request will be sent to your manager for approval. You will receive an email notification once your request has been approved or rejected.

### 3.2. Viewing Your Leave History

You can view a complete history of all your leave requests on the **Leave History** page. This includes:

- **Pending Requests:** Requests that are awaiting approval.
- **Approved Requests:** Requests that have been approved by your manager.
- **Rejected Requests:** Requests that have been rejected.
- **Cancelled Requests:** Requests that you have cancelled.

### 3.3. Checking Your Leave Balance

The **Leave Balance** page shows you a detailed breakdown of your leave entitlement and balance for each leave type.

- **Entitlement:** The total number of leave days you are entitled to for the year.
- **Taken:** The number of leave days you have already taken.
- **Balance:** The number of leave days you have remaining.

## 4. Team Calendar

The **Team Calendar** provides a view of your team's leave schedule. This can help you plan your own leave and coordinate with your colleagues.

- **View by Month:** See who is on leave for the current month.
- **Filter by Department:** If you are a manager, you can filter the calendar to view the schedule for different departments.

## 5. Notifications

You will receive email notifications for the following events:

- **Leave Request Submitted:** Confirmation that your leave request has been successfully submitted.
- **Leave Request Approved:** Notification that your leave request has been approved.
- **Leave Request Rejected:** Notification that your leave request has been rejected, along with the reason.
- **Leave Request Cancelled:** Confirmation that you have successfully cancelled a leave request.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I cancel a leave request?**

A: On the **Leave History** page, you can cancel any pending leave requests by clicking the "Cancel" button.

**Q: What happens if I don't have enough leave balance?**

A: The system will not allow you to submit a leave request if you do not have sufficient balance for the selected leave type.

**Q: Who approves my leave requests?**

A: Your leave requests are sent to your assigned manager for approval. If you do not have a manager assigned, the request will be sent to the default administrator.

**Q: How are public holidays and weekends handled?**

A: The system is configured by your administrator to either include or exclude public holidays and weekends from leave calculations. Please check with your administrator for details on your company's policy.

For any other questions, please contact your administrator or HR department.
